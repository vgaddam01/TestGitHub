Flight search results page - Shop by Schedule tab

Step 1: Filter the results (if desired)

There are two sets of filters: one at the top of the page and one on the left side of the page.

Left side of the page: Use the up and down arrows (show and hide) as needed.

  • In the Change Search area, change your arrival or departure cities; your dates, times, or time ranges; class of service and desired airline (depending on your company's configuration); or the refundability (if available). Click Search. Travel shows the new results on the page.

    Note Once you have used the filter area, the Previous Searches area appears at the top. Once you have made a change, you can use this area to return to your original results.

  • Use the sliders to narrow your search.

  • In the Display Settings area, select any of the available options.

  • Use the Airport Filters boxes to filter results for airports. Travel shows the lowest price for each airport. Once you filter unwanted airports by clearing the check box, the list displays only results for the selected airports. Filtering unwanted results makes the display more manageable.

  • In the Connecting Airport Filters area, select the desired connecting airport(s).

Matrix - Top right side of the page: Use the grid to filter the results.

  • To see flights with a certain number of stops, click the cell with the number of stops. The search results below will reflect that choice.

  • To see flights for a particular airline, click the cell for that airline. The search results below will reflect that choice.

  • To see flights with the selected number of stops and a specific airline, click the appropriate cell. The search results below will reflect that choice.

  • To see the rates in a different currency, use the currency converter in the upper-right corner.

Step 2: Sort the results (if desired)

Below the matrix are the Shop by Fares and Shop by Schedule tabs. To the right of the tabs is the Sorted By list. Select the desired sort option.

Step 3: Review the search results - Shop by Schedule tab

The search results appear on the Outbound tab and the Return tab. The following information appears:

  • Price (or starting price), airline/carrier, times, class of service, plane type, and total time

  • The View seats link which allows you to view seat availability (depending on the airline)

  • Also:

    • Hover the mouse pointer over any airport code or airline name to see the full name for the airport or airline.

    • Additional icons may appear. Hover the mouse pointer over an icon to see a description.

To choose an outbound and return flight:

  1. Click the blue "select" button for the desired outbound flight. Then:

    • That flight "floats" to the area above the Outbound and Return tabs. Additional flight information is provided.

    • The Return tab is automatically displayed, showing the available return flights.

  2. Click the blue "select" button for the desired return flight. It, too, moves to the top of the page as well.

  3. Click Show all details along with the other links and buttons available to gather information about seats, rules, fares, etc.

To move a selected flight back to the search results, either:

  • Click Remove. - or -

  • Click another flight from the search results. It replaces the existing flight.

Step 4: Select the flight

  1. Click the blue "price" button. (Note that the shape and color of the icon to the left of the blue button reflects policy compliance.) The Review and Reserve Flight page appears.

    Note If the flight is out of policy, you may have to provide a reason.

  2. Navigate through the page and:

    • Verify or modify the traveler information and frequent traveler program information.

    • Select a seat, if available.

    • Review the price information.

    • Select a form of payment, if available, as defined by your company's configuration.

    • Review the fare rules.

  3. Click Reserve Flight and Continue.

  4. Then:

    • If you requested the flight using the Flight (or Mixed Flight/Train) Search tab and you elected to reserve a hotel room or car, Travel now displays those search result pages.

    • If not:

      • If you have elected to book for one or more companions, you may be asked to specify the method of payment for the companion(s). Click Next when done.

      • Your Travel Details page (itinerary) appears.

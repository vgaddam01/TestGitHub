Company Car Registration

Use the Company Car Registration page of Profile to enter information about your company car.

Note

  • Depending on your company's configuration, your company may register company cars for you.

  • Depending on your company's configuration, some of these options may not be available to you. Check with your Concur administrator.

Access

To access this option, click Profile > Profile Settings > Company Car (left menu).

Register a car

To register a car:

  1. On the Company Car Registration page, click Register a New Car.

  2. Enter the required information, as defined by your company.

  3. Click OK.

Delete a car

To delete a car:

  1. On the Company Car Registration page, select the check box to the left of the car that you want to delete.

  2. Click Delete.

Selecting the per diem location

Depending on your company's configuration, you may be asked to select the per diem location for your hotel so that Travel can present to proper rates.

  1. On the Search for locations near page, select the country. Select CONUS for "Continental US."

    Note The choice you make here determines how the next list is populated.

  2. Select the state or region.

    Note The choice you make here determines how the next list is populated.

  3. Select the city or area. The rates appear.

  4. Click Next.

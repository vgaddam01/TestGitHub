Cash Advance Admin - overview

Note

  IMPORTANT -- Feature Access and Permissions

Your company may or may not use this feature.

If your company uses this feature, you may or may not have the correct permissions to access it. You may have limited permissions, for example, you can use only certain options (view but not create or edit).

If you need to use this feature but do not have the proper permissions, contact your company's Expense administrator.

The Cash Advance Administrator is in charge of creating and issuing cash advances to users. Cash Advance administrators use the Cash Advance Admin tool to create the cash advance and issue it to the desired user. The user must have a cash advance account code assigned on the User Details page to receive a cash advance. Once the Cash Advance administrator issues the cash advance in Expense, the company pays the actual cash to the employee. Eventually, the employee must create an expense report to account for the cash.

The Cash Advance Admin tool is used by a client user/administrator with the Can Administer Cash Advance role. With the Cash Advance Admin tool, the administrator can:

  • Search for employees to view their cash advances and history

  • Search for a cash advance

  • View cash advance details

  • Issue cash advances

  • Record manually-returned cash amounts from an employee

Images - Overview

If your company uses Concur's imaging service, you may have the following options available for including receipts or images with your payment requests:

  • You can fax your receipts/invoices. You can print a Fax Receipt Cover Page from within Invoice and then fax it and your receipts/invoices. For more information about faxing, see Fax receipt images for a payment request.

  • You can upload scanned images of your receipts/invoices as PNG, JPG, JPEG, PDF, HTML, TIF, OR TIFF files. For more information about uploading images, see Attach scanned receipt images for a payment request.
